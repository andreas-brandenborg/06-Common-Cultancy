Russia Invades Ukraine

Thursday, February 24th 2022, Ukraine is invaded by Russia.
The media is flooded with posts, prayers, and fundraisers - the World wants to help!
Years go by and the war continues; unfortunately, media coverage does not. While Ukraine fights on against the largest country on Earth, social media moves on.

The purpose of this website is to highlight the development of social media interactions on posts concerning the war in Ukraine.


Created by Andreas Brandenborg, Magnus Giemsa, Laurits Munk, and Victor Lotz.

2022

February 24, 2022: Russia launches a full-scale invasion of Ukraine.

March 2022: Heavy fighting continues in various regions, including Kyiv and Kharkiv.

April 2022: Russia captures the southern city of Mariupol after a prolonged siege.

August 2022: Ukrainian forces launch a counteroffensive, recapturing significant territories in the Kharkiv region.

November 2022: The conflict enters a stalemate with both sides holding their positions.

2023

June 2023: Ukraine launches a major counteroffensive, making gains in the southern regions.

August 2023: Continued Ukrainian advances, but the conflict remains intense.

November 2023: Winter campaigns begin, with both sides focusing on fortifying positions.

2024

March 2024: Increased use of drones and missiles due to shortages of artillery ammunition.

April 2024: Ukrainian forces make further gains in the spring campaigns.

August 2024: Russian forces launch summer-autumn offensives, but Ukrainian defenses hold strong.

December 2024: The war continues with no resolution in sight, and both sides suffer heavy casualties.
